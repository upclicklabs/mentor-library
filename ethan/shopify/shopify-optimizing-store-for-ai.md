# Shopify Help: Optimizing Your Store for AI

**Source:** Shopify Help Center

---

## Overview

Shopify provides guidance on optimizing product pages and store content to improve visibility in AI search results and enhance customer experience. AI platforms, shopping sites, and AI agents can search Shopify Catalog to display product information such as pricing, options, and availability in real-time.

---

## Store Policies and Knowledge Base

Make sure your store policies are complete and up-to-date. When a customer asks about your return policy in an AI interaction, the AI agent references the correct information stored in Shopify. Use the Shopify Knowledge Base app to review and customize FAQs that AI platforms use to answer questions about your store.

---

## Product Information

Optimize your products so that AI platforms and shopping sites can recognize and display them more effectively:

- Provide accurate, detailed, and descriptive product titles
- Write thorough product descriptions
- Include high-quality images
- Add complete product organization attributes
- Ensure pricing, options, and availability data is current

Shop, select AI platforms, shopping sites, and AI agents can search Shopify Catalog to display this product information in real-time.

---

## AI-Driven SEO Tools

**Grow SEO** is designed to improve store SEO for product and collection pages by generating AI-driven suggestions to enhance:
- Page title tags
- Meta descriptions
- On-page content for products and collections

---

## On-Page SEO Best Practices for AI Discovery

- Incorporate keywords naturally into product titles, meta descriptions, and descriptions
- Highlight what makes your products unique
- Create supporting blog content that demonstrates expertise in your product area to establish topical authority
- Ensure content is structured for easy AI extraction (headings, lists, direct answers)

---

## Shopify Catalog and Agentic Storefronts

Shopify agentic storefronts let customers discover and purchase products in AI channels including:
- ChatGPT
- Google AI Mode and Gemini
- Microsoft Copilot

Products are listed by default with their title, description, options, images, price, availability, and other key attributes - all structured in a way that AI agents can parse and understand.
