# The Agentic Commerce Platform: Shopify Connects Any Merchant to Every AI Conversation

## Overview

Shopify has announced a comprehensive strategy to establish itself as the backbone of agentic commerce, enabling merchants to sell directly within AI conversations across major platforms. This initiative represents a significant shift in how commerce will function in the AI era.

## Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP)

Shopify, in collaboration with Google, has introduced the Universal Commerce Protocol — an open standard designed to facilitate seamless commerce transactions through AI agents. The protocol has already garnered endorsement from over 20 retailers and platforms.

### Key UCP Capabilities

The protocol allows customers to:

- Submit discount codes
- Input loyalty credentials
- Select preferred subscription billing options
- Confirm selling terms (final sale, pre-order timing)

It works with any payment processor, including Shopify Payments, and supports multiple integration approaches: REST, Model Context Protocol (MCP), Agent Payments Protocol (AP2), and Agent2Agent (A2A) protocols.

## Google Integration

Shopify merchants can now sell directly in AI Mode within Google Search and the Gemini app through an embedded experience managed from the Shopify Admin. Select merchants are eligible for Google's Direct Offers pilot, enabling exclusive deals to appear directly in AI Mode conversations.

## Microsoft Copilot Integration

Shopify has released an update to its Microsoft Copilot integration featuring Copilot Checkout — a new embedded checkout experience allowing users to shop within Copilot itself.

## Agentic Plan

For the first time, Shopify has opened its catalog to brands not using Shopify storefronts through a new Agentic plan. This allows any brand to:

- List products in Shopify Catalog
- Access billions of products categorized through specialized AI
- Sell on AI channels, the Shop app, and future Shopify partners
- Set up product data once for distribution everywhere

## Participating Brands

Early adopters include Monos, Gymshark, Everlane, Keen, and Pura Vida, who are leveraging these new channels to reach customers at critical purchase moments.

## Key Technical Details

### Protocol Support

The UCP supports multiple integration methods, making it flexible for different technical stacks:

- **REST API** — Traditional API integration
- **Model Context Protocol (MCP)** — For AI model interactions
- **Agent Payments Protocol (AP2)** — Specialized payment flows for AI agents
- **Agent2Agent (A2A)** — Direct agent-to-agent communication

### Commerce Infrastructure

This positions Shopify as the infrastructure layer supporting commerce across any conversational interface — whether in search, assistants, productivity tools, or emerging platforms. The key insight: commerce in the AI era needs a universal protocol, and Shopify is building it.

## Strategic Implications for AEO

- **AI agents will soon complete purchases**, not just recommend products — optimizing for AI visibility directly translates to revenue
