# Shopify Catalog: Mapping Your Product Data Sources (Default Listing)

**Source:** Shopify Help Center

---

## Overview

When products are syndicated to AI channels by Shopify Catalog, they are listed by default with their title, description, options, images, price, availability, and other key attributes - all structured in a way that AI agents can parse and understand.

All products that meet the requirements for Shopify Catalog are automatically included by default.

---

## What AI Platforms Receive by Default

Shopify Catalog provides AI platforms with:

- **Product title** - Used for matching AI queries
- **Description** - Provides context and details for AI recommendations
- **Options** - Variants like size, color, material
- **Images** - Visual product representation
- **Price** - Current pricing data
- **Availability** - In-stock / out-of-stock status
- **Other key attributes** - Category, brand, SKU, etc.

AI platforms, shopping sites, and AI agents can search Shopify Catalog to display this information in real-time.

---

## Catalog Mapping for Custom Data

You can impact how your products are displayed to potential customers in AI chats by completing optional tasks with **Shopify Catalog Mapping**:

- Review how your key product data is delivered to Shopify Catalog
- Change the sources for relevant product data by mapping to metafields and structured data
- Override default data sources with richer, more specific product information

This is particularly valuable for:
- Products with detailed specifications that don't fit standard fields
- Stores with custom metafields containing richer product data
- Merchants wanting to surface specific differentiators in AI conversations

---

## AI Platforms Supported

Shopify agentic storefronts connect to:
- **ChatGPT** - Direct product discovery and instant checkout
- **Google AI Mode and Gemini** - Product recommendations in AI search
- **Microsoft Copilot** - Shopping assistance via AI
