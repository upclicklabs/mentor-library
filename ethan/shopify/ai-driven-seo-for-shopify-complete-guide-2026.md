## The Shift to Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)

AEO is the practice of structuring content and site signals so that answer systems can identify your brand as a dependable source for direct, accurate, extractable, and contextually useful answers. This includes:

- Google AI Overviews
- Google AI Mode
- Voice interfaces
- Chat-based discovery
- LLM-driven tools that cite or reference web content

AEO is not a replacement for SEO. It is the discipline of making your brand, website, and content easy for answer systems to understand, extract, cite, and trust.

## Critical Shopify-Specific AEO Strategies

### Data Quality First

Clean, structured, machine-readable product data gives AI the raw material it needs to recommend you. Focus on:
- Complete, descriptive product titles
- Thorough product descriptions with use cases
- Accurate pricing and availability
- Proper category assignments
- Google Product Category codes

### Technical Layers

Stack these on top of SEO fundamentals:
- **Product feed enrollment** - Submit feeds to Google, ChatGPT Merchant Program
- **llms.txt files** - Provide AI-readable store context
- **Crawler-specific access rules** - Allow AI bots in robots.txt (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot)

### Content Strategy

- Optimize for the specific questions Shopify buyers ask AI
- Build topic clusters around product categories
- Create comparison content (your product vs. alternatives)
- Develop use-case specific landing pages

### Local SEO for AI

For NZ and AU markets specifically:
- Localize product descriptions with regional terminology
- Build local citations and review profiles
- Ensure pricing is in local currency
- Optimize for local shipping and availability signals
