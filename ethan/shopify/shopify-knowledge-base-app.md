# Shopify Knowledge Base App

**Source:** Shopify Help Center

---

## Overview

The Shopify Knowledge Base app helps optimize your business presence across AI shopping platforms and improves customer self-service using automatically generated and customizable store facts.

The app is a **free first-party app available with all Shopify plans**.

---

## What the App Does

When shoppers use AI agents to search for products or ask about store policies, answers are curated based on your store's catalog and settings. The Knowledge Base app lets you:

- View customer questions about your store
- Customize a set of AI-readable FAQs
- Review automatically generated facts about your business
- Customize answers or add your own Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Key Features

### FAQ Management

- View and manage FAQs that AI platforms use to answer customer questions
- Auto-generated FAQs are based on your store's existing data
- Add, edit, or delete individual questions and answers
- FAQs are not displayed directly on your storefront - they serve as a trusted data source for AI platforms

### Store Policies Integration

- Store policies (returns, shipping, etc.) feed into the Knowledge Base
- Ensure policies are complete and up-to-date so AI agents can reference accurate information
- When customers ask about policies via AI chat, the answers come from your Knowledge Base data

### AI Performance Analytics

The app provides data on:
- How often AI shopping agents query your store data
- Which products are being surfaced in AI shopping conversations
- What questions customers are asking through AI channels
- FAQ metric performance over time

---

## Why This Matters for AI Discovery

Your FAQs and store facts become the source of truth that AI platforms reference when answering questions about your brand. This means:

- Accurate, complete FAQs = accurate AI responses about your store
- Outdated or missing info = AI giving customers wrong answers
- The Knowledge Base is your direct channel to influence what AI says about you
