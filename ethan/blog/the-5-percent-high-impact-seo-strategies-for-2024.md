# The 5%: High Impact SEO Strategies for 2024

## Overview

This content features a video presentation by Ethan Smith, CEO of Graphite, discussing high-impact SEO initiatives for 2024. The core premise addresses a common business challenge: It's common for companies to fall into the SEO Death Spiral, which occurs when companies work on low impact activities that drive no results.

## Key Content Areas

**Video Focus:**
The presentation covers three primary domains:
- Technical SEO
- Editorial SEO
- Programmatic SEO

**Approach:**
Strategies are grounded in research papers and experimental results from Graphite's client work, ensuring evidence-based recommendations rather than theoretical approaches.

## About the Presenter

Ethan Smith serves as CEO of Graphite, a research-focused growth agency. His background includes:
- Adjunct professor at IE Business School
- Instructor for SEO and AEO courses at Reforge
- Prior research experience at Microsoft Research Lab Cambridge and UC Santa Barbara, focusing on human-computer interaction and psychology
