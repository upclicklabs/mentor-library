# The Future of Search: LLMs, AI Search and Optimization Strategies

## Overview

This content features a video discussion between Ethan Smith (CEO) and Gregory Druck (Chief AI Officer) at Graphite, exploring artificial intelligence's impact on search.

## Key Topics Covered

The session addresses several critical areas:

- **AI Answer Engines**: Strategies for optimizing content in AI-powered search systems
- **Large Language Models (LLMs)**: Understanding how LLMs function and their role in modern search
- **Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)**: Technical foundations powering AI search capabilities
- **Answer Optimization**: Moving beyond traditional SEO toward content designed for AI systems
- **Citation Optimization**: Ensuring proper attribution when content appears in AI outputs
- **Content Surfaces**: Coverage across platforms including ChatGPT, Perplexity, and others

## About the Speakers

**Gregory Druck, PhD** - Chief AI Officer at Graphite, previously served as Chief Data Scientist at Yummly and Postdoctoral Scientist at Yahoo! Research. He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from UMass Amherst.

**Ethan Smith** - CEO of Graphite; adjunct professor at IE Business School teaching SEO and AEO through Reforge. His background includes research at Microsoft Research and UC Santa Barbara.
