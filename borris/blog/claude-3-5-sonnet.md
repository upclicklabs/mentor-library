# Introducing Claude 3.5 Sonnet

**Date:** June 21, 2024

Anthropic launched Claude 3.5 Sonnet as the first release in the Claude 3.5 model family. The model raises the industry bar for intelligence, outperforming competitor models and Claude 3 Opus on a wide range of evaluations.

## Availability and Pricing

The model is available for free on Claude.ai and the iOS app, with higher rate limits for paid subscribers. It's accessible through the Anthropic API, Amazon Bedrock, and Google Cloud's Vertex AI at $3 per million input tokens and $15 per million output tokens, with a 200K token context window.

## Performance Highlights

Claude 3.5 Sonnet operates at twice the speed of Claude 3 Opus while maintaining competitive pricing. In coding evaluations, Claude 3.5 Sonnet solved 64% of problems, outperforming Claude 3 Opus which solved 38%.

## Vision Capabilities

The model demonstrates significant improvements in visual reasoning tasks and can accurately transcribe text from imperfect images.

## New Features

Anthropic introduced Artifacts, allowing users to see, edit, and build upon AI-generated code and content in real-time within Claude.ai.

## Safety and Privacy

Red teaming assessments concluded the model remains at ASL-2 safety level. The company emphasizes it doesn't train on user data without explicit permission.
