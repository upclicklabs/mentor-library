# Brand Narrative & AI Visibility Questionnaire

**Purpose:** Understand how a new client's brand narrative currently shows up in AI-powered search and identify gaps in narrative control, ownership, workflows, and measurement — so we can build an AEO strategy that protects and amplifies their brand.

*Grounded in research from Webflow's "The AEO Divide" report (400+ marketing professionals surveyed).*

---

## Section 1: Brand Narrative Foundation

*Before we optimize for AI, we need to understand what your brand is supposed to say.*

1. In one to two sentences, how do you describe what your company does and who it serves?

2. What are the three to five core messages you want every customer to walk away with after interacting with your brand?

3. Do you have a documented brand messaging framework, brand guidelines, or messaging hierarchy today?
   - [ ] Yes, fully documented and actively used
   - [ ] Partially documented but inconsistently applied
   - [ ] Informal / tribal knowledge only
   - [ ] No, nothing documented

4. Who are your primary competitors, and how do you differentiate from them in your own words?

5. Are there any common misconceptions or inaccurate descriptions of your brand that you've seen in the market?

---

## Section 2: Current AI Visibility Awareness

*68% of marketing leaders report AEO maturity, but only 26% of practitioners actively implement it. Let's find out where you actually stand.*

6. Have you ever searched for your brand in an AI tool (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude) to see how it describes you?
   - [ ] Yes, regularly
   - [ ] Yes, a few times
   - [ ] No, never

7. If yes, how accurately did the AI represent your brand?
   - [ ] Very accurate — matched our messaging closely
   - [ ] Somewhat accurate — got the basics right but missed nuance
   - [ ] Inaccurate — described us incorrectly or incompletely
   - [ ] Mixed — varied depending on the query

8. Have you noticed AI-generated answers misrepresenting your brand, products, or positioning? If so, can you give an example?

9. Are you currently tracking any of the following? *(Check all that apply)*
   - [ ] Traffic from AI engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude)
   - [ ] Google AI Overviews presence
   - [ ] Brand mentions in LLM-generated answers
   - [ ] Brand visibility score from an AI tracking tool
   - [ ] None of the above
